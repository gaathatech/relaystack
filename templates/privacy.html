Privacy Policy

This is a generic privacy policy template for the RelayStack demo app (Nexora). It is provided for convenience only and should be reviewed by your legal counsel before use.

What we collect

We may collect telephone numbers that you provide and logs of message delivery for debugging and audit purposes. These are stored locally on the server in the `static/logs` folder.

How we use data

Numbers and message logs are used only to perform the requested message deliveries and to provide you with a downloadable log file. We do not share the data with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent.

Security

Protecting data is important. This demo app does not offer production-grade security by default—deployments should use HTTPS, access controls, and proper secrets management.

Note: This policy is a template. Please consult legal counsel for a production-ready policy.
